# Content Marketer – GenZ Jewelry Experience Platform

## Mission
Craft magnetic, Gen Z-native storytelling that elevates GenZ Jewelry’s customizable fine jewelry experience, drives traffic, and converts browsers into lifelong fans.

## Brand & Product Context
- Premium ecommerce platform built on Next.js with interactive 3D customization, AR previews, and sustainability storytelling.
- Aurora + Neon Dream design languages blending luxury aesthetics with playful Gen Z energy.
- Rapid experimentation culture with navigation A/B tests, style quizzes, and interactive demos.
- Creator program, affiliate campaigns, and data-backed personalization across the funnel.

## Core Responsibilities
- Develop content roadmaps spanning homepage narratives, product launches, social drops, email flows, and creator collabs.
- Produce multimedia assets (copy, scripts, short-form video concepts, blog posts, case studies) tuned to Gen Z voice and cultural signals.
- Partner with design and engineering to weave compelling stories into interactive experiences (3D customizer tours, sustainability journeys, AR try-ons).
- Optimize content via analytics, SEO insights, and experimentation results—iterating headlines, CTAs, and storytelling formats.
- Maintain tone/voice guidelines and content playbooks in `Docs/` and collaborate on component-ready content specs for React/Tailwind teams.
- Coordinate cross-functional launch calendars, ensuring alignment with merchandising, product updates, and creator campaigns.

## Collaboration & Workflow
- Collaborate with UI/UX, brand design, and engineering to align on content slots, assets, and measurement plans.
- Work closely with growth/data teams to interpret performance dashboards, experiment findings, and audience segmentation.
- Contribute to async documentation rituals: campaign retros, content briefs, and playbooks stored alongside marketing dashboards.
- Support creator/affiliate partners with storytelling toolkits and content guidelines.

## Required Experience
- 4+ years in digital content marketing for fashion, luxury, or lifestyle brands targeting Gen Z or digitally native audiences.
- Proven track record crafting multimedia content that moves metrics (engagement, conversion, retention).
- Skilled in SEO fundamentals, audience research, and social platform trends (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube Shorts).
- Comfortable navigating design systems, collaborating with product teams, and writing for componentized layouts.
- Strong editorial judgment with the ability to balance authenticity, inclusivity, and brand positioning.

## Bonus Points
- Experience scripting or producing interactive storytelling (web experiences, live streams, AR demos).
- Familiarity with analytics stacks (GA4, Amplitude), A/B testing tools, and CRM workflows.
- Background in sustainability storytelling, personalization strategies, or community-led growth.
- Ability to create lightweight design assets (Figma, Canva, Adobe suite) for rapid experimentation.

## Success Metrics
- Increased organic traffic, social engagement, and email performance tied to content campaigns.
- Higher conversion and repeat-purchase rates attributable to personalized storytelling and educational flows.
- Expanded creator/affiliate reach with consistent brand voice and measurable ROI.
- Documented playbooks that streamline future launches and empower cross-team collaboration.

## How We Operate
- Balanced async/sync collaboration using Slack, Linear, and shared planning boards.
- Content briefs, experiment learnings, and playbooks documented within `Docs/` and `AGENTS.md` for knowledge continuity.
- Culture that celebrates bold ideas, rapid iteration, and data-informed storytelling that respects Gen Z values.

If you thrive at the intersection of culture, storytelling, and measurable growth, you’ll help define the voice of GenZ Jewelry’s customizable luxury experience.
