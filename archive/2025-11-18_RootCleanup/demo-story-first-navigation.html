Story-led navigation

Guide visitors through the GlowGlitch universe before they ever scroll.

The Story-First navigation introduces visitors to the brand narrative, then channels them toward collections, customization, content, and concierge care. Each mega-panel is capped at six tiles so scanning stays effortless, and imagery mirrors the homepage hero for instant recognition.

6 Tiles max per panel
30s To grasp the brand story
1 Primary CTA per intent
Creator spotlight panel highlighted Seasonal imagery swaps keep the nav fresh without touching layout code.

What stakeholders should review

Hover through the navigation to confirm copy, imagery, and destinations. The cards below preview how marketing, CX, and merchandising collaborate inside the Story-First framework.

Marketing Narrative Ensure Discover and Journal tiles have owners, copy decks, and imagery. Use this pass to flag any missing pages or placeholders before development.
Merchandising Focus Confirm the Shop panel ladders up to current campaign priorities and that Spotlight Capsules sync with homepage hero inventory.
Support & CX Validate concierge SLAs, booking flows, and help-center anchors so the Help panel launches with accurate promises.
