Inline Filter Concepts

Option 1 · Accordion Rail

The pill row becomes a single accordion—tap a pill to expand its content inline; tapping another automatically closes the previous one. The expanded area inherits the same width as the card so nothing overflows on mobile.

Option 2 · Pinned Slot

The Refine Details card keeps a dedicated “details slot” under the pills. Whatever filter you choose fills the slot; switching pills swaps the content. This keeps the interaction clear and avoids stacked panels.

Price Range

Option 3 · Inline Sheet

The entire Refine Details block expands into a sheet when a pill is active. The sheet shows the selected filter form and pushes the grid down, and closing it collapses back to the compact view.
