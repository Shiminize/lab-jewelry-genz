Caring for Your Ring

To maintain the beauty and brilliance of your ring, follow these care instructions:

  • Clean regularly with warm water and mild soap
  • Use a soft-bristled brush for gentle scrubbing
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and ultrasonic cleaners
  • Store separately to prevent scratching
  • Remove during exercise and manual activities

We recommend professional cleaning and inspection annually to maintain optimal condition.
