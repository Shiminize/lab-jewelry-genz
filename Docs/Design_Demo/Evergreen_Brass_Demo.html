Evergreen Brass

Evergreen Brass translates the Shihara Lab evergreen charcoal and jewelry brass highlights into the Gen Z Jewelry system. Tokens here map directly to app/styles/tokens.css, ensuring Tailwind utilities powered by our --ja-* variables stay synchronized without touching component markup.

Palette Anchors

--color-bg #F9F8F6 · Page background
--color-surface #FFFFFF · Content panels
--color-ink #111111 · Headline text
--color-ink-2 #3F4A45 · Body text
--accent-secondary #FAC151 · CTA highlight
--accent-muted #EDE4CE · Muted badge/accent BG

Type & Copy Sample

“Minimal evergreen grounding with a brass edge.”

Evergreen Brass leans into Shihara Lab’s refined charcoal base while warming moments like CTAs or announcements with a polished brass sheen. Neutral text pairs maintain AA contrast on --color-surface and --color-bg.

Jewelry Drop

Verde Column Hoops

Sculpted in 18k gold, the Verde Column Hoops echo the palette’s brass highlight while the deep evergreen grounds the frame.
