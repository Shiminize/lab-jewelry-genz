Palette Anchors Primary: #3F4A45 No gradients

Quiet Evergreen Core

A matte evergreen primary (#3F4A45) sits against soft porcelain backgrounds and buff accents. Buttons lean into a contrast sandwich: light call-to-action foregrounds, translucent secondary, and tonal tertiary for utility states.

Canvas #F4F1EC

Warm off-white keeps product photography luminous. Reserve for page backgrounds and wide sections. In Tailwind, map to --color-bg.

Surface #FFFFFF

Crisp panel surface for cards and modals. Pair with subtle shadows and 1px outlines using #E8E3DC.

Primary #3F4A45

Hero typographic color, primary buttons, and key dividers. Drives AA contrast on light surfaces. Map to --color-ink and --color-accent.

Accent Soft #D6C6B2

Jewelry-inspired buff tone for badges, cards, and soft callouts. Use as --accent-secondary.

Muted Ink #8A978F

Secondary typography and icons. Keeps copy readable while respecting the muted palette.

Outline #E8E3DC

Soft border tone for cards and input outlines. Pair with 12px radius to maintain the calm aesthetic.

Primary Actions & Messaging

“Evergreen poise with quiet luxury highlights.”

Amplify hero headers with the primary ink, mix muted copy for supporting text, and maintain breathing room through off-white surfaces. Buttons follow a light/dark pairing for clarity across states.

Implementation Notes

  • Map --color-bg → #F4F1EC, --color-surface → #FFFFFF.
  • Set --color-ink and --color-accent to #3F4A45, keep --color-ink-2 at #8A978F.
  • Use #D6C6B2 for --ja-accent-secondary and #E8E3DC for --color-line-subtle.
  • No gradients: rely on flat fills, layered shadows, and tonal contrast for depth.
