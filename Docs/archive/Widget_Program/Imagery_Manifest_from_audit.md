# Imagery Manifest from Audit

Guidance for producing on-brand images across key pages. Maintain the luxury, architectural, geometric, calm aesthetic with ivory grounds and deep green accents only (current theme: ivory #F4F1EC, deep green #3F4A45). Use zero-radius framing, hairline borders, soft sculptural shadows, and calm directional light.

## Home (/)

### Home – Hero | Editorial Lead
- **Section selector (for reference):** `section.relative.section-spacing-none.bg-app.HeroSection_wrapper___Q8wA`  
- **Image filename suggestion:** `home-hero-architectural.avif`
- **Scene description:**  
  Architectural still life on an ivory set: travertine blocks and linen backdrop with one hero ring and pendant arranged in a rectilinear grid. A single deep-green silk ribbon threads through as an accent. Cool, soft directional light casts gentle, sculptural shadows; no gradients or glow.

- **Brand rules to respect:**
  - Zero-radius (no rounded corners)
  - Ivory / soft white background
  - Deep green only as an accent
  - Soft sculptural shadow, no glow
  - Calm, directional light (no harsh gradients)

- **Aspect ratio:** `21:9`  
- **Target resolutions:**
  - Desktop: **1920 px** (max source width: 2880 px)
  - Mobile: **390 px** (max source width: 828 px)

- **Recommended formats:** AVIF first, then WebP, JPEG fallback  
- **File size budget:** ≤ 220–250 KB (hero, above the fold)

- **Alt text to use in the site:**  
  "Still life of a gold ring and pendant arranged on stone blocks with a deep green silk accent."

- **Notes for the art team:**  
  - Keep the deep green ribbon minimal and linear.  
  - Use hairline deep-green border if a frame is visible; no rounded corners.  
  - Above the fold: ensure sharp focus and reserved aspect to avoid cropping issues.
